Dear Real Economy advocate,

after 15 years since advent of Bitcoin, crypto community has not yet use cases in the mainstream. For different reasons, existing tokens are not suitable as a medium of exchange. For centuries, the recognised medium of exchange has been representative money. To fill up this gap, and bring crypto to the mainstream, we are creating the first ever Representative Digital Money. For the first time in the crypto era, we aim for the decentralization in value for currencies (as non-fiat stablecoins).

To provide the first use case, we leverage the inefficiencies and up to 50% overcosts of the global 1B cylinders per month LPG retail market to introduce crypto payments on the real economy.

Key facts:

  • To build a private currency competition based on representative digital money.
  • Target market is initially LATAM. LATAM is a commodities and natural resources rich market with 668M consumers booming on crypto.
  • MVP is built and ready for beta testing with a real use case
  • Start bottom-up in Peru, scaling to regions, asset payments and commodities
  • Team has 25y experience in oil and gas regulation and IT. Also experience in financial regulation and compliance and monetary theory.
  • Agreement with large retailers is neither required nor desirable.

The project aims for a new paradigm based on competition of private representative currencies. We are looking for a 6 x $50k private token sale from likeminded investors to run the FUNDING stage of the project.

Catallactic

We learn from $CYGAS benchmark to define the principles and implement CryptoCommodity. CryptoCommodity aims to build a framework, community, know-how and tools to create representative money backed by real value. CryptoCommodity is the first decentralized token model supporting the Regression Theorem of Mises.
A new tokenization model

Catallactic aims to create representative money as non-fiat commodities backed stablecoins, capturing the marginal utility of the underlying asset as perceived by consumers.

Catallactic also aims to build the principles of a Quality Theory of Money to enable a healthy money competition.
VISIT CATALLACTIC

Please, note that, whereas representative money has an stabilization mechanism (as regular stablecoins), representative money is not fiat money (unlike commodities price pegged stablecoins as PAXG). And, as representative money does not build on top of the fiat system, it provides a protection of value for fiat debasement.

How will we do it?

Big LPG retailers use their orders information and marketing capability to increase prices and offer low quality work. LPG uberification will provide to specialized independent retailers the information that they need and rewarding work whereas reducing market prices and increasing coverage.

Overview on traction and operational tasks:

  1. Setup an small physical retailer/shop to deliver gas in Lima. A trivial task: renting an small office space to hold LPG stock, agree with a wholesaler, buy some moto, hire a couple of retailers and get a government license. There are 16k LPG retailers in Peru.
  2. Start delivering LPG in Lima will provide huge benefits to the project: (1) stream of income to guarantee project execution (2) validation of apps and processes (3) introduction of CryptoCommodity (4) users and a real use case for all the next steps in the roadmap. No agreements with big retailers are needed.
  3. Validate the MVP apps (already developed and alpha tested) with real deliveries. Beta testing in real cases is needed
  4. Setup a filling plant if suitable and convenient (Optional resource)
  5. Run an ICO up to $6M.

Overview on planned scalations:

  1. Scale to more retailers.
  2. Scale to alternative payments and DeFi
  3. Scale to other countries (see below)
  4. Scale to other commodities/utilities (Catallactic)
