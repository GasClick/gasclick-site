Tokenizing Energy

Crypto tokenization is the process of converting something of value into a digital token that's usable on a blockchain application. In the context of LPG delivery, we will convert LPG orders into tokens.

As a LPG order is a fungible good, in order to capture a persistent value, we will tokenize repeatable orders. Typically, we can assume that a gas cylinder provides value for 1 month to an average family, so we can make the assumption that our token will capture a monthly value of a LPG cylinder. This will also help to stabilize the supply of our currency.

A match made in haven

CRYPTOGAS Architecture

Decentralization

CryptoGas is been built on the Principles of Decentralization. Decentralization is a multifaceted feature. CryptoGas is the only token that is created on top of a RWA marketplace, and therefore, with a built-in price formation mechanism. As a consequence, CryptoGas is the only token that can be fulfill the Unit of Account requirement as a Decentralization Feature. In other words, differently to Stablecoins, CryptoGas wont depend on any other currency, including fiat.

Upgradeable

Complying with Decentralization Principles is a complex task and must be done on an iterative approach. This is why CryptoGas must be an upgradeable token. Maybe, in future, governed by a DAO.

Compliance

CryptoGas must also comply with Regulatory Legal Frameworks. ERC3643 looks to provide a good starting point for this requirement.
