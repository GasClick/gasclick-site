Buy CRYPTOGAS

At the moment, we are running funding rounds so not yet in the exchanges. During this period you can buy CRYPTOGAS as described below:

Metamask

1. Step: Install Metamask

Go to Metamask and install the Chrome extension. Follow their steps from the guide and finish the setup.

Account

2. Step: Create a Wallet on Metamask

Once you have setup metamask, you can create a wallet. follow the instructions provided here. Do not share your seed phrase with anyone and store them in physical storage.

ETH

3. Step: Send ETH, BNB, MATIC or USDT to your wallet

Once you have created your wallet, you can send ETH, BNB, MATIC or USDT from an exchange like Kraken, Coinbase or Binance to your Metamask address or use any onRamp tool to buy tokens.

CYGAS

4. Step: Swap your ETH for CYGAS

Now you can swap ETH in your wallet for CYGAS. Select the amount of CYGAS to purchase and click Purchase button to finalize your trade.
